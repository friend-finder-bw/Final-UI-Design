What is Friend Finder?

Friend Finder is a multifaceted app that aims to connect like-minded people to create meaningful friendships. Affectionately described by the New York Times as "platonic Tinder," Friend Finder takes the guesswork out of making friends. Instead of relying on fate, users of Friend Finder are able to take initiative and seek out like-minded people in their area.
Say goodbye to loneliness and hello to adventures with new friends!

How Does it Work?

Using our sophisticated matching algorithm, Friend Finder provides users with a stack of potential friends based on information inputed by the user. Factors like age, hobbies, likes, dislikes, and interests are used to determine the likelihood of compatibility. Users are able to adjust factors based on their inherent importance to create an individually tailored friend finding experience.
